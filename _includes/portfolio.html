Attitude Control Crazyflie
Click here to read this article!

This paper proposes the development of a system for the control of the orientation (roll, pitch and yaw angles) of an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), of the quadrotor type. This system has six degrees of freedom, being classified as an underactuated mechanical system. For simulation purposes, the mathematical modeling of the quadrotor Crazyflie 2.0 was based on Newton-Euler formalism.
